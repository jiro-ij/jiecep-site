1st General Assembly: 2023-204
MSU-GSC Chapter

September 6, 2023

The JIECEP MSU-GSC successfully conducted its 1st General Assembly on September 6, 2023, at the Mindanao State University - General Santos City, Library-AVR. During the event, JIECEP MSU-GSC students actively participated in various activities organized by the officers. Additionally, they had the opportunity to listen to inspirational speeches delivered by Engr. Tanya Molarto-Osorio, PECE, MEng-ECE, our ECE Program Coordinator; Engr. April C. Sur, the JIECEP Adviser; and Engr. Venus T. Elevencionado, RME, ME-EE, the EEE Chairperson. The students were also inspired by a discussion led by our resource speaker, Engr. Jio R. Alayon, about real-world experiences, opportunities, and challenges faced by Electronics and Communications Engineers.

It is an obligatory matter to conduct a general assembly per semester to foster transparency and good relationship within the organization, JIECEP – MSU – GSC. On September 06, 2023, the General Assembly for the 1st Semester of the academic year 2023 – 2024 took place. The said assembly took place at the Library AVR of Mindanao State University – General Santos City at 1:00 pm up until 5:00 pm. With this, open discussions regarding matters within the organization and, most especially, the financial report were talked about during the general assembly.
